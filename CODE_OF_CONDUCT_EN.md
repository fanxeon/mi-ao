# Code of Conduct

[中文](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) · [English](CODE_OF_CONDUCT_EN.md)

We want project discussions to remain friendly, professional and evidence-based.

Participants must respect different backgrounds and levels of experience, and keep discussions focused on code, protocols, reproduction steps and user impact. Harassment, discrimination, disclosure of another person's private information, and publication of unredacted recordings or device identities are not accepted.

Maintainers may edit or remove content that violates these rules and restrict participation when necessary. Report security or privacy issues privately according to [SECURITY_EN.md](SECURITY_EN.md).
